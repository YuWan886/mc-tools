This gunpack is for the Timeless and Classic Guns: Zero mod and has been created and playtested only for version 1.20.1 (Forge)

 

Description:

This pack adds only one gun, the lore accurate P-06, a triple burst charging sniper rifle with its familiar delay and charge up, capable of dealing high damage to nearly one-hit almost any entity. Comes with the Elo-Sight to make hitting targets simpler.

 

Installation:

2. Open your game folder, find the TACZ folder, and put the zip.file inside.
3. Restart the game, and ez to go.
